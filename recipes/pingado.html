Pingado

Ingredients:

  • Coffee
  • Milk

Steps:

  1. Heat the milk in the stove.
  2. Once the milk is close to boiling, turn the stove off and fill 3/4 of a cup with it.
  3. Add a drop of coffee, filling the last 1/4 of the cup.

Pingados are usually served in what Brazlians call an American glass, if you would like one of those, make sure to buy one here when you visit Brazil.
