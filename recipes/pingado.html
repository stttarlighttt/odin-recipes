Pingado

The pingado is the most popular way of drinking coffee in Brazil. It is made with just the right amount of coffee and milk. The drink tastes creamy because of the milk, but the coffee's strong flavor isn't lost. It's a must for all coffee lovers.

Ingredients:

  • Coffee
  • Milk

Steps:

  1. Heat the milk until it's close to boiling.
  2. Fill 3/4 of a cup with the warm milk.
  3. Add coffee to fill the last 1/4 of the cup.

A pingado is usually served in what Brazilians call an American glass, if you would like one of those, make sure to buy one here when you visit the country.
