Pão na chapa

Ingredients:

  • Bread roll
  • Butter

Steps:

  1. Cut the bread roll.
  2. Spread butter on both sides of the bread. This is the crunchiness secret.
  3. Turn on the stove and let the fridge hit up a bit.
  4. Position the slides on the fridge and keep applying pressure with a fork.
