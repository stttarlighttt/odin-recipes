Pão na chapa

A pão na chapa can be found in the menu of almost every Brazilian bakery. What makes it particularly delicious is the fine crunchy crost of butter that forms with its contact with the hot pan surface. The more butter, the better the taste.

Ingredients:

  • Bread roll
  • Butter

Steps:

  1. Cut the bread roll in half.
  2. Spread butter on the internal side of both slices.
  3. Turn on the stove and let the pan heat up.
  4. Position the slices on the pan, then, keep applying pressure with a fork.
